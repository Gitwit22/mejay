1. Overview

Welcome to MEJay! We're committed to protecting your privacy and giving you control over your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use our music app.

Your Privacy Matters: We believe in transparency and your right to control your data. This policy is written in plain language to help you understand exactly what happens with your information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and password (encrypted)
  • Profile Data: Any optional profile information you choose to provide
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through Stripe (we don't store credit card details)
  • Communications: Messages you send us through support or contact forms

2.2 Information We Automatically Collect

  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app (features used, songs played, playlists created)
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, browser type
  • Audio Files: Metadata from music files you upload (title, artist, duration, BPM)
  • Performance Data: App performance metrics to improve functionality

2.3 Information We Don't Collect

  • We don't track you across other websites or apps
  • We don't sell your personal information to third parties
  • We don't access your music library without your permission
  • We don't use invasive advertising trackers

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 To Provide Our Service

  • Create and manage your account
  • Store your playlists, preferences, and music library
  • Process BPM detection and tempo matching
  • Enable Party Mode and shared features
  • Sync your data across devices

3.2 To Improve Our Service

  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance features
  • Fix bugs and improve performance
  • Develop new features based on user behavior
  • Test and optimize the user experience

3.3 To Communicate With You

  • Send important service updates and announcements
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Send promotional emails (only with your consent, and you can opt out anytime)
  • Notify you about account or subscription changes

3.4 For Security & Legal Compliance

  • Protect against fraud and abuse
  • Enforce our Terms of Service
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes and troubleshoot problems

4. Data Storage & Security

4.1 Where Your Data Lives

Your data is stored securely on Cloudflare's infrastructure:

  • Cloudflare D1: Database for user accounts and playlists
  • Cloudflare R2: Storage for uploaded music files
  • Local Storage: Some preferences are stored locally on your device

Location: Data is stored in Cloudflare's global network, which may include servers in various countries.

4.2 How We Protect Your Data

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest
  • Password Security: Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms
  • Access Controls: Strict limits on who can access user data
  • Regular Security Audits: We continuously monitor for vulnerabilities
  • Secure Payment Processing: Payments handled exclusively by Stripe (PCI-compliant)

4.3 Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: We retain your data as long as your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: Data is permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion
  • Legal Requirements: Some data may be retained longer if required by law
  • Backups: Backups are retained for up to 90 days for disaster recovery

5. Information Sharing

5.1 We Share Information With:

  • Stripe: For payment processing (they have their own privacy policy)
  • Cloudflare: Our hosting and infrastructure provider
  • Service Providers: Third parties who help us operate the app (under strict confidentiality agreements)

5.2 We May Share Information When:

  • Required by law or legal process
  • Necessary to protect our rights or property
  • You explicitly consent to sharing
  • In connection with a business transfer (acquisition, merger, etc.)

5.3 We Never:

  • Sell your personal information to advertisers or data brokers
  • Share your music library or playlists without permission
  • Use your data for unauthorized marketing purposes

6. Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

6.1 Access & Data Portability

  • Access: Request a copy of all data we have about you
  • Data Export: Download your playlists and account information

6.2 Correction & Deletion

  • Update: Edit your profile and account information anytime
  • Delete: Request complete deletion of your account and data

6.3 Control & Opt-Out

  • Email Preferences: Unsubscribe from marketing emails
  • Data Processing: Object to certain types of data processing
  • Third-Party Access: Revoke access to third-party integrations

6.4 How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@mejay.app or through the in-app settings. We'll respond within 30 days.

Regional Rights: If you're in the EU (GDPR), California (CCPA), or other regions with specific privacy laws, you may have additional rights. Contact us to learn more.

7. Children's Privacy

MEJay is not intended for children under 13 years old. We don't knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you're a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at privacy@mejay.app, and we'll delete it.

Age Verification: By using MEJay, you confirm that you're at least 13 years old (or the minimum age required in your country).

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements.

When We Update This Policy:

  • We'll update the "Effective Date" at the top
  • We'll notify you through the app or via email
  • For major changes, we'll ask for your consent
  • The updated policy will be posted on our website

Your Continued Use: By continuing to use MEJay after changes take effect, you accept the updated Privacy Policy.
